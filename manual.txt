enter your equations in the blue box.

to multiply:
e.g. 3*3

to get the power of an integer:
e.g. 3**3

to get the mod of a number:
e.g. 10%2

to divide a number:
e.g. 3/3

use parenthesis to enter full equations:
e.g. ((3-1)**3)*5

for hamming codes:
enter an integer
e.g. 45
select the bit size and even/odd then hit enter
